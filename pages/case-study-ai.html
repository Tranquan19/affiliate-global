My Daily AI Workflow: A Tuesday in the Life

Every day looks a little different, but my core routine stays the same. I've broken it into four blocks. Here's what a typical Tuesday looks like using my AI tool stack:

7:00 AM

Morning Briefing & Content Planning

I open Notion AI and review my content calendar for the week. I check which keywords are trending, what my competitors published, and which gaps exist in my editorial pipeline. Notion AI generates content briefs in seconds — target keyword, outline structure, angle, and suggested internal links. I approve or tweak each brief. Takes 20 minutes instead of the 2 hours it used to take.

Notion AI 15 min saved
7:30 AM

Morning Writing Block — Jasper

With my content briefs ready, I jump into Jasper and use the Blog Post workflow with Boss Mode. I paste the brief, set the tone, and let Jasper generate a 2,000+ word first draft. I go section by section, feeding it my personal anecdotes and data points as custom instructions. The full draft lands in about 15 minutes. I then spend 30 minutes editing, fact-checking, and adding my unique voice. One complete, publish-ready post in 45 minutes — down from 5 hours.

Jasper 4 hrs saved
10:00 AM

Late Morning — WriteSonic Bulk Content

By late morning I'm working on shorter content: email newsletters, social media captions, product descriptions. WriteSonic is faster than Jasper for short-form, so I batch-produce 10-15 pieces of micro-content at once. I use their templates for email sequences, Twitter threads, and Facebook ad copy. Another 2 hours of content done in 30 minutes total.

WriteSonic 1.5 hrs saved
1:00 PM

Afternoon — Synthesia Video Production

This is where the real magic happens. I take the blog post I wrote earlier and feed it into Synthesia with a custom AI avatar. I paste the script, pick an avatar who matches my brand voice, and choose a background. Synthesia generates a high-quality video with natural lip-syncing, body language, and consistent audio. I do a quick review, fix a few awkward phrasings, and export. A full explainer video that used to take an entire afternoon now takes 45 minutes from script to export.

Synthesia 3 hrs saved
3:00 PM

Mid-Afternoon — Pictory Video Clips

For social media, I load the same blog post into Pictory and let it auto-extract the best 3-5 key points. It generates short-form clips (60-90 seconds each) with stock footage, captions, and voiceover — perfect for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. I export 4 clips in about 20 minutes. These perform 2x better than my manually edited shorts ever did.

Late Afternoon — Organize & Schedule

Back in Notion, I move everything to the "Ready to Publish" column. I schedule blog posts in WordPress, queue videos in YouTube Studio, and batch-schedule 2 weeks of social posts. The whole pipeline is visible on one dashboard — nothing falls through the cracks. I review analytics from the previous week, identify what's working, and brief Notion AI for tomorrow's batch.
