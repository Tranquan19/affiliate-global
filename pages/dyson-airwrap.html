Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dyson Airwrap worth the money?

Yes, if you regularly style your hair (3+ times/week). The Airwrap reduces heat damage by 50% and lasts 7-10 years — that's ~$65-90/year, cheaper than regular salon visits. The Shark FlexStyle is a solid alternative at half the price.

What's the difference between Dyson Airwrap and Shark FlexStyle?

Main differences: Price ($599 vs $199-329), Smart features (Airwrap has i.d. app + smart temp sensor), Durability (7-10 years vs 3-5), and Attachments (6+ vs 5). Shark can double as a hair dryer, Airwrap cannot.

Can Dyson Airwrap replace a hair dryer?

Not completely. Airwrap requires 80% dry hair to work effectively. You'll still need a separate hair dryer (like the Dyson Supersonic) for full drying.

Which is better for short hair: Dyson or Shark?

Both work well. Dyson Airwrap has a slight edge with its 20mm small barrel and root volumizing brush specifically designed for short hair.
